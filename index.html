About

I'm a sound engineering & audio production student currently studying at NTU with a focus on Drum & Bass music. My work spans music production, live sound, and creative direction — built around a passion for high-energy electronic music and the culture around it.

I'm currently developing both my technical skillset and a long-term strategy for entering the music industry — combining creative output with an understanding of the business, legal, and commercial frameworks that underpin professional practice.

Alongside my studies, I'm the founder and primary creative force behind HAZMAT — an anonymous DnB artist project built around an anonymous mascot concept, inspired by acts like Marshmello and Daft Punk.
